The Chorale operates almost entirely off of generous recurring memberships and one-time donations from individuals and businesses in the community. Thank you!!

100% of your donation goes towards furthering the Chorale's work. The Chorale uses donation funds for ongoing expenses such as purchasing music, renting a performance venue, and compensating staff.

At a concert, 70% of donations are shared with the selected local charity and 30% of donations support the Chorale.

Chorale Memberships (Monthly Donation)

Support the Chorale's ongoing mission as an official member. As a token of our gratitude, you will receive rewards at each concert for your generosity.

Basic Membership - $10/month

  • Entry into every raffle
  • No registration fee upon joining an ensemble

Chorale Membership - $30/month

  • Everything from Basic, plus...
  • 1 Themed T-shirt from every semester
  • Up to 2 ads in every program

Silver Membership - $50/month

  • Everything from Chorale, plus...
  • One copy of every piece performed, including original compositions
  • All themed T-shirts from each semester

Gold Membership - $100/month

  • Everything from Silver, plus...
  • Official sponsorship status
  • Company presence in all of our programs
  • Free advertisement on website
  • Etc.
