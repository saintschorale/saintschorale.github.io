The Chorale operates almost entirely off of generous donations from individuals and businesses in the community. Thank you!!

100% of your donation goes towards furthering the Chorale's work. The Chorale uses donation funds for ongoing expenses such as purchasing music, renting a performance venue, and compensating staff.

Donations given at a concert are split 70/30 for the benefit of the concert's selected charity.
