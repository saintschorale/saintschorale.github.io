Joining the Chorale

We are always looking for new singers, directors, and administrative staff. In your email, please include the following:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • A short description of your musical experience
  • Briefly share why you want to join the Chorale
  • An updated resume [only Prospective Directors/Staff]

Sponsoring the Chorale

The Chorale operates almost entirely off of generous donations from individuals and businesses in the community. Visit our Donate page to setup a one-time/recurring donation.

If you wish to discuss a more significant sponsorship opportunity please email us with the following details.

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number of sponsoring entity)
  • The estimated dollar value of the sponsorship
  • Describe the details of the sponsorship -- how does it differ from the opportunities on our Donate page?
